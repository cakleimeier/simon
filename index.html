Welcome to Simon!

Here's a take on the classic memory game of Simon. Each round, a pattern plays on the board's buttons, you must match the pattern during your turn. Every round adds one more button to the pattern.

There are TWO mode: normal and strict. In NORMAL mode, a mistake causes the pattern to replay. In STRICT mode, a mistake ends the game. If you switch modes, you start a new game.

Beat all 20 rounds to win!
